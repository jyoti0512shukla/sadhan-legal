Privacy Policy

Sadhana ("the App") is a Hindu spiritual practice app developed as a personal devotional companion. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Spiritual practice data — Japa session counts, reading progress, streaks, and milestones
  • Personal reflections — Daily journal entries you create within the App
  • Preferences — Tab order, selected mantras, Panchang location, ambient sound settings, and other customization choices
  • Bookmarks and reading plans — Your saved positions and active reading plans
  • Story progress — Katha reading progress, favorites, and downloaded story content cached locally on your device
  • AI chat history — Conversations with the Sadhana AI Bot are stored on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, chat messages may also be synced to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit (see "iCloud Sync" below)
  • Purchase status — Whether you have unlocked Premium is cached locally for fast access; the authoritative record is maintained by Apple through StoreKit

iCloud Sync (CloudKit)

The App uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your AI chat history across your devices via your iCloud account. This data is stored in your private iCloud container — only you can access it. We cannot read, access, or retrieve your synced data. iCloud sync is automatic if you are signed into iCloud on your device; no separate login is required. If you delete the App, synced data remains in your iCloud account and will be restored if you reinstall.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools
  • We do not store personal data on our own servers (AI chat and iCloud sync are handled by Google and Apple respectively — see details below)

Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions, each used solely for the stated purpose:

PermissionPurpose
MicrophoneAarti sing-along feature — listens to your singing to highlight the current lyric line. Audio is processed on-device and is never recorded or transmitted.

Network Usage

The App may connect to the internet for the following purposes:

  • Sadhana AI Bot — When you use the AI chat feature, your messages are sent to Google's Gemini AI model via Firebase AI Logic to generate responses. Google processes these messages to provide answers and may retain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Data Privacy. The AI Bot is scoped to dharmic/spiritual topics only. Do not share sensitive personal information in chat.
  • iCloud Sync — AI chat messages are synced to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit, enabling chat history to persist across app reinstalls and sync across your devices
  • Scripture content — Download optional scripture content packs (e.g., Vedas, Mahabharata) from GitHub
  • Aarti & Chalisa catalog — Fetch the latest aarti and chalisa texts
  • Stories catalog — Download the stories catalog and individual story content from GitHub
  • Live Darshan — Load temple live-stream information and embed YouTube video players for in-app darshan viewing
  • In-App Purchases — Communicate with Apple's App Store servers to process Premium unlock purchases and verify entitlements

The App functions fully offline for all bundled content (Panchatantra stories, Ramcharitmanas, Bhagavad Gita, Panchang, and more). The AI Bot requires an internet connection.

Data Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's standard frameworks (SwiftData). It is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. We have no access to your data.

Data Retention & Deletion

You control your data entirely. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data from your device. AI chat messages synced to iCloud via CloudKit will remain in your iCloud account and will be restored if you reinstall the App. To permanently delete synced chat data, use the "Clear Conversation" option in the AI Bot before uninstalling, or manage your iCloud storage through iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any user, making it safe for individuals of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The following third-party services are used for specific features:

  • Google Firebase AI Logic (Gemini) — Powers the Sadhana AI Bot. When you send a message to the AI Bot, your message text and recent conversation history are transmitted to Google's servers for processing. Google may process this data per their Privacy Policy. No other personal data (name, device ID, location) is sent. The AI Bot is an optional feature — you are never required to use it.
  • Apple CloudKit (iCloud) — Syncs AI chat messages to your private iCloud account. This data is encrypted and stored by Apple in your personal iCloud container. Only you can access it; we have no ability to read your synced data. Apple's Privacy Policy applies.
  • GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) — Hosts downloadable scripture files, story content, and catalog data. No personal data is sent; only content files are fetched.
  • YouTube (youtube.com) — Live Darshan temple streams are embedded using YouTube's standard embed player. YouTube's own privacy policy applies to video playback. No user data is shared by the App with YouTube.
  • Apple StoreKit — Processes in-app purchases for Premium unlock and voluntary donations. Transactions are handled entirely by Apple; the App does not collect or store payment information.

Crash Diagnostics (MetricKit)

The App uses Apple's built-in MetricKit framework to receive crash and hang diagnostics for stability improvements. This data is processed locally on your device. When you have "Share with App Developers" or "Share iPhone Analytics" enabled in iOS Settings, similar diagnostic data may be shared with Apple and made available to developers through Xcode Organizer. We do not send this data to our own servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
